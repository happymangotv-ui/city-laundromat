Our Story

From the Neighborhood
to All of Manhattan

The City Laundromat has served the Bronx community for years. What started as a neighborhood laundromat at 391 Brook Avenue has grown into a full-service operation with Manhattan pickup and delivery. We built our reputation on quality, reliability, and treating every customer like family.

Our mission is simple: give New Yorkers their time back. Whether you drop off a bag at our Bronx location or schedule a pickup from your home, we handle the work so you don't have to.
